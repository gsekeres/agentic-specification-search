## Key Notes

1. **Black academic outcomes robust**: GPA, exams passed, continuation all positive and significant across control variations and sample restrictions.
2. **White IAT effect moderate**: Significant with full controls (p=0.03) but sensitive to some specifications (HC3 SE: p=0.056).
3. **Many baseline outcomes tested**: Tables 3-5 cover IAT, academic, friendship, attitudes, communication, social outcomes -- 28 baseline specs.
4. **Placebo tests pass**: All 6 baseline outcome placebo tests are insignificant.
5. **Year 2 persistence**: Black academic effects persist into second year.
6. **Small sample concern**: White student subsample ~117, limiting power for heterogeneity.
7. **Multiple testing**: With 148 specs and many outcomes, some results likely significant by chance.
